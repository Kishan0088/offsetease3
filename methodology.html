A cycle built to deliver now — and compound over time.

Every engagement runs on the same five-stage cycle: Assess, Strategise, Implement, Measure, Improve. The discipline is what makes the work defensible to auditors, investors and the board.

In detail

The five stages, with what each delivers.

01

Assess

Defensible baseline. We measure full footprints, map exposure across jurisdictions, benchmark peers using the methodologies you'll be benchmarked on, and quantify the financial implications of inaction.

  • GHG inventory to GHG Protocol
  • Regulatory exposure register
  • Peer benchmarking
  • Financial impact modelling
02

Strategise

Funded pathway. Marginal abatement modelled, sequenced for finance-ability. Targets calibrated to be ambitious enough to be credible and feasible enough to be funded.

  • Marginal abatement cost curve
  • Pathway scenarios
  • Target architecture
  • Capital and financing model
03

Implement

Systems, data, delivery. Project structuring, technology procurement, supplier engagement, financing arrangement, and the data systems that make next year's disclosure cheaper than this year's.

  • Programme management
  • Data architecture build
  • Supplier engagement
  • Disclosure preparation
04

Measure

Audit-ready performance. Instrumentation against milestones in the language investors and auditors recognise — limited or reasonable assurance.

  • KPI instrumentation
  • Control framework
  • Audit-period support
  • Investor narrative
05

Improve

Compounding progress. Findings closed, ambition raised where it becomes affordable, architecture extended to absorb the next regulatory move at lower marginal cost.

  • Cycle-over-cycle improvement
  • Ambition recalibration
  • New regulation absorption
  • Capability transfer
